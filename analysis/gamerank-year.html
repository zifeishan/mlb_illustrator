What is GameRank

A ranking algorithm to measure the ability of pichers and batters!

Intuition from PageRank and HITS.

If a batter wins over good pitchers when hitting, then he is a good batter; if a pitcher wins over good batters when pitching, then he is a good pitcher.

How to use

First, select the year of the game; then, select the team. Then you can see the visualization results. If you need, you can change the ranking metric.

Nodes with black borders are pitchers; nodes with white borders are batters.

Nodes with the same color inside are in the same team.

Yellow edges indicates that a batter makes a successful attack; blue edges indicates that a pitcher makes a successful defend.
